Recipe Cooking Instructions

Prep: 5 mins | Cook: 10 mins

Ingredients

Heavy cream 1/2 cup
Aged Parmesan 1/4 cup
Fresh Spinach 2 cups
Garlic 2 cloves
Olive Oil 1 tbsp
Salt 1/2 tsp
Black Pepper 1/4 tsp

Instructions

1.

Mince the garlic and thoroughly wash the spinach.

2.

Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Sauté garlic for 1 minute until fragrant.

3.

Add spinach and cook until wilted, about 2-3 minutes.

4.

Lower the heat. Stir in your heavy cream and simmer for 2 minutes to thicken slightly.

5.

Remove from heat, fold in parmesan until melted, and season with salt and pepper.

Recipe Cooking Instructions

Prep: 10 mins | Cook: 10 mins

Ingredients

Firm Tofu 14 oz
Brown Rice 1/2 cup
Fresh Broccoli 1 cup
Soy Sauce 2 tbsp
Sesame Oil 1 tbsp
Fresh Ginger 1 tsp
Fresh Garlic 1 tsp

Instructions

1.

Press and drain the tofu, then cut into 1-inch cubes.

2.

Chop broccoli into bite-sized florets and mince garlic and ginger.

3.

Heat sesame oil in a wok. Fry tofu for 6-8 mins until crispy on all sides. Remove from pan.

4.

Sauté broccoli, garlic, and ginger until tender-crisp. Whisk soy sauce and pour over.

5.

Add tofu back to the pan, toss to coat, and serve hot over your brown rice.
